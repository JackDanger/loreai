### Decision

<!-- lore:019ebc96-83cc-7986-8f4a-666630801d76 -->
* **Pause in-flight curation during active conversation to prevent system\[2] cache busts**: Pausing in-flight curation during active conversations prevents system\[2] cache busts. Any mid-session knowledge change re-renders system\[2] (context-bound LTM), busting the full downstream prompt cache (~549K tokens). Legitimate knowledge changes are acceptable at session boundaries (idle path) only. The \`inFlight\` flag re-enables mid-session curation — treat as footgun for cache-sensitive providers (Anthropic); only safe for free-write providers (e.g. MiniMax) where cache busting has no cost. Must be documented with explicit warning in config.ts. The volatile delta channel is the correct long-term solution for fresh mid-session knowledge on cache-sensitive providers.
