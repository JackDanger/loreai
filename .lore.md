* **Background LLM rate limiting: p-limit(2) + 429 circuit breaker in background-limiter.ts**: Global concurrency limit for background LLM work in \`packages/gateway/src/background-limiter.ts\`. Uses \`p-limit(2)\` to cap simultaneous background LLM calls across all idle sessions. Circuit breaker trips on 429 responses and pauses all background work for the \`Retry-After\` duration. ### Decision

<!-- lore:019e596c-e3a8-7229-bca2-92c0fe79e268 -->
* **Budget throttling uses proxy sleep / gradual slowdown, not hard 429 blocks**: Budget throttling uses proxy sleep (invisible delay) not hard 429 blocks. Rationale: flat 60s/120s Retry-After rejected as 'super annoying'; 429 triggers unpredictable client retry behavior; proxy sleep is invisible — upstream just appears slower; 3-5s sleep imperceptible during normal coding turn. Only conversation turns (Case 3 in pipeline.ts) throttled; meta requests and compaction exempt. Background workers excluded — their fraction of total costs is not meaningful to throttle. The throttle exploits gateway-level cross-request visibility to apply gentle friction without triggering client retry machinery.
