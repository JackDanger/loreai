* **cache-economics.ts: #947 meta-aware coolBust adjustment — probability-weighted intra-session bust cost**: Issue #947 fix. The original \`coolBust\` formula assumes the compressed prefix stays small for the resumed-session horizon. A mid-flight meta-distillation destroys that advantage: the remaining \`futureTurns - T\` turns (where T is the bust time) pay \`full · read\` instead of \`compressed · read\`. New terms (additive to coolBust only — hold-warm and cool-full-write are NOT adjusted): \`expectedBusts = clamp01(futureTurns / metaThreshold)\` (probability of at least one intra-session bust); \`bustCostPerBust = (futureTurns / 2) × (full - compressed) × read + metaDistillCostPerCall\`; \`metaBustCost = pReturn × expectedBusts × bustCostPerBust\` (Seer finding: pReturn factor for consistency with other return-conditional terms); \`coolBust_actual = coolBust_baseline + metaBustCost\`. The \`futureTurns / 2\` is the integral of \`futureTurns - T\` over \`T ∈ [0, futureTurns]\`, divided by \`futureTurns\` — the expected remaining-turns-after-bust under a uniform-bust-time assumption. Two gates: (1) \`metaThreshold > 0\` (caller actually configured meta — default 0 → byte-identical to pre-#947, back-compat preserved); (2) \`compressed < full\` (compaction advantage exists to lose — otherwise adjustment would create a phantom bust and violate the \`coolBustCost === coolFullWriteCost\` invariant). The earlier \`futureTurns > 0\` gate was removed — mutation testing confirmed it was dead code (\`finiteNonNeg\` floors futureTurns to 0, and \`clamp01(0/metaThreshold) = 0\` collapses the term). Result struct adds \`expectedMetaBusts\` and \`metaBustCost\` for Sentry/observability — logged in the cache-warmer log line (cache-warmer.ts:1302) so they're not dead code. Caller \`evaluateCacheStrategy\` (gradient.ts:768) threads \`metaThreshold = cfg.distillation.metaThreshold\` and computes \`metaDistillCostPerCall\` via the new pure helper \`estimateMetaDistillCostPerCall(workerModel, metaThreshold)\` in cache-economics.ts. \`AVG\_SEGMENT\_TOKENS = 3000\` and \`META\_OUTPUT\_TOKENS = 2048\` are documented approximations, not measured — the model is directionally correct, ±2× on real segment sizes. 🔴 Tie-break invariant preserved: pure-additive adjustment cannot introduce a tie. 🔴 No-compaction invariant preserved: \`compressed === full\` → \`metaBustCost = 0\` → \`coolBustCost === coolFullWriteCost\`. Production evidence (session 0AVWKugtmhBKqLOX, F=120k, C=80k, pReturn=0.75, f=15, metaThreshold=20): expectedBusts=0.75, bustCostPerBust=300,000, metaBustCost=168,750 (≈10% of coolBust baseline = 1,620,000). Per the test in cache-economics.test.ts:393-414, the decision flips from cool-bust to hold-warm when meta-aware under high expectedBusts (F=100k, C=50k, p=0.95, f=10, metaThreshold=2 → 0.95×1.0×250,000=237,500 added → coolBust adjusted 1,282,500 > holdWarm 1,145,000).
